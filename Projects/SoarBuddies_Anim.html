    • Soar Buddies

    Description

    An independently produced postgraduate 3D animated short film and thesis research project, completed from initial concept through final production. Responsible for all aspects of development, including story creation, asset production, rigging, animation, lighting, rendering, compositing, and research documentation. Certain aircraft designs in this film draw artistic inspiration from commercial model kits by Tiger Model, Hasegawa’s “Egg Plane” series and Freedom Model Kit, without affiliation or endorsement.

    Software
